Baby Keem

Carrer

Keem began pursuing music more seriously at age 15. His first track on SoundCloud “Options” was released in November 2016. He followed up with “Kissing Friends” and “FWM” in 2017. All of his early drops were credited as Hykeem Carter. In addition to SoundCloud, he’s also had success on the Audiomack platform. Keem dropped his debut EP, Midnight, in January 2018. It featured 9 songs that he previously recorded. The entire project was recorded out of his room.
